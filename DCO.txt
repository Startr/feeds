How to sign off

Add a "Signed-off-by" line to every commit message using your real name:

    Signed-off-by: Jane Doe <jane@example.com>

The easiest way is to pass `-s` to `git commit`:

    git commit -s -m "Fix the thing"

This adds your sign-off automatically using your configured `user.name` and
`user.email`. If either is missing, `git` will refuse to commit until you
set them.

By signing off, you are asserting the four clauses above for that
contribution. The sign-off is a simple statement, not a legal waiver, and
it does not transfer copyright. You keep copyright on your contributions.
